About the Platform

This website serves as an educational tool to illustrate the inner workings of algorithms, including processes such as swapping, sorting, searching, and more. Its primary aim is to assist students in grasping algorithmic concepts with ease and enhancing their understanding.

Developers

The Algorithm Visualizer website is a collaborative effort by Mohd Anas and Arshad Ahmed. With a focus on educational utility, our intention is to provide students with a resource that facilitates a clear and thorough understanding of algorithms.

Features

  • Interactive algorithm visualization
  • User-friendly interface

Usage Guidelines

This platform is specifically designed to help students comprehend algorithms effortlessly and enhance their conceptual clarity. To make the most of the website, follow these steps:

  1. Select a specific algorithm from the menu.
  2. Adjust parameters as needed.
  3. Initiate the visualization process by clicking "Visualize."
